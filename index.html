The Hearts Behind the Whisk

Sown by Siblings,
Washed by the Waves

Born from a shared passion for high-end patisserie and the rhythmic waves of Kampung Tok Jembal, Chocacake is the vision of three siblings who believe that every golden layer of pastry tells a story of home.

Our journey began amidst the hustle of the local night market, where we spent nights perfecting our sourdough and kneading dough by hand while the coastal breeze carried the scent of fresh bakes. Today, we’ve traded the market stall for a beachside sanctuary, inviting you to savor the buttery, artisanal flavors we’ve perfected together over the years.
